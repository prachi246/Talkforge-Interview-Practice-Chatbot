🎙️ TalkForge – AI Interview Practice Chatbot

📌 Overview

TalkForge is an AI-powered interview practice platform designed to help users improve their communication, interview performance, and spoken English through realistic AI-driven conversations. The application provides HR and technical interview simulations, random conversation practice, and AI-generated feedback to help users build confidence and enhance their communication skills.

⸻

✨ Features

*  AI-powered HR and Technical Interview Practice
*  Random Conversation Mode for improving spoken English and fluency
*  Real-time AI-generated feedback on interview responses
*  Clarity, Fluency, and Pronunciation Analysis
*  Grammar Corrections and Vocabulary Suggestions
*  Filler Word Detection
*  Session History and Performance Tracking
*  XP, Gems, Streak System, and Leaderboard
*  Secure User Authentication with Firebase
